# Demo Script — Target 2:35

## 0:00–0:15 — Problem
"AI can answer almost any question, but an answer is not proof that someone
learned. Most learning chats also disappear without helping anybody else."

## 0:15–0:28 — Product
"ConceptLab AI creates a complete learning loop: Learn, Build, Test, Teach Back,
and Share."

## 0:28–0:45 — Generate
On screen, enter **Binary Search**, choose Beginner, and click **Build my lab**.

Voice:
"GPT-5.6 generates a level-appropriate explanation, analogy, key ideas, a visual
model, a runnable experiment, and a formative quiz."

## 0:45–1:15 — Explore
Scroll through the explanation, Mermaid diagram, and Python experiment.

Voice:
"The learner does not only read. They inspect a model and receive code whose
parameters they can change to observe the concept."

## 1:15–1:32 — Quiz
Answer the three questions and click **Check answers**.

Voice:
"The quick check provides retrieval practice, but ConceptLab goes further."

## 1:32–2:03 — Teach Back
Paste:
"Binary search finds a value in a sorted list by checking the middle item. If
the target is smaller, it repeats on the left half; if larger, on the right
half. Each comparison removes half of the remaining search space, so its time
complexity is logarithmic."

Click **Evaluate understanding**.

Voice:
"The learner teaches the idea back. GPT-5.6 evaluates the actual reasoning,
identifies strengths and gaps, and produces a next challenge. This is evidence
of understanding, not merely content consumption."

## 2:03–2:25 — Community
Click **Publish Learning Card** and show the card in the community section.

Voice:
"With explicit permission, the completed lab becomes a reusable Learning Card.
Future learners can discover it, and the roadmap adds forking and collaborative
improvement."

## 2:25–2:48 — Codex and close
Show the repository briefly.

Voice:
"Codex helped turn the product specification into the working FastAPI app,
connect the full interface, refine structured prompts, handle errors, and
document setup. ConceptLab AI transforms isolated AI tutoring into a learning
community where knowledge compounds."

## Recording checklist
- Public YouTube visibility
- Under 3:00
- Clear audio
- Show the working product, not slides only
- Explicitly say **Codex** and **GPT-5.6**
- Briefly show repository and README
